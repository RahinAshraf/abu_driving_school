Welcome to Abu's Driving School Hertfordshire, where Abu, your dedicated instructor, offers personalised driving lessons to help you master the road.

Why Learn with Us?

  • Abu is a certified instructor with extensive experience in teaching driving.
  • We offer the advanced driving course: Pass Plus.
  • We offer Block Booking and also train our pupils on the test routes used by the examiners, so that our pupils are in a great position to pass their driving tests.
  • We offer both Manual and Automatic lessons.

Whether you're just starting or aiming to improve your skills, Abu will guide you every step of the way to ensure you become a confident and capable driver.

About

Abu's Driving School has proudly served Hertfordshire for several years. We are dedicated to supporting every pupil in reaching their driving objectives and passing their test with confidence.

Meet Abu

Abu is a certified and highly skilled driving instructor with a true passion for teaching. With a track record of helping hundreds of pupils pass their driving tests, Abu blends a deep knowledge of driving techniques with a warm, encouraging approach to teaching.
