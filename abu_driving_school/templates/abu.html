About

Abu's Driving School has proudly served the Hertfordshire for several years. Our goal is to deliver top-notch driving lessons that are both educational and enjoyable. We are dedicated to supporting every student in reaching their driving objectives and passing their test with confidence.

We offer block booking and also train our students on the test-routes used by the examiners, so that our students are in a great position to pass their driving tests!

Meet Abu

Abu is a certified and highly skilled driving instructor with a true passion for teaching. With a track record of helping hundreds of students pass their driving tests, Abu blends a deep knowledge of driving techniques with a warm, encouraging approach to instruction.
